I did consider that; in fact, it was my intention for everyone to realize that, so that there would be this interesting WIFOM-y dynamic of object-passing -- Town wants to pass to the Towniest player, but also doesn't want to pass to a player that's going to die; Mafia wants to kill not only threats to their victory but also people who might get a lot of objects. In addition, I think this object-passing system is a decent way to root out lurkers and force scum to actively participate to win -- if Mafia's going to try to win, they not only have to pass under the radar, they have to actually make an effort to appear Townish in order to get objects.

It did take some thinking with the objects' own powers and how to distribute the objects to balance it right -- in fact I don't think I did the best job of it, but I like the way it's turning out.
